about us

Once upon a time, there was a ventriloquist named Jack who had a dummy named Charlie. Jack and Charlie were the best of friends and performed together every night. One day, Jack got sick and couldn't perform, so Charlie had to go on stage alone. To everyone's surprise, Charlie started talking on his own and became a huge hit. Jack was jealous of Charlie's newfound fame and decided to get rid of him. He threw Charlie in the trash, but the next day, Charlie was back on stage, alive and well. It turned out that Charlie was a magical dummy who could come to life. From that day on, Jack and Charlie performed together as equals, and they became the most famous ventriloquist duo in the world.
